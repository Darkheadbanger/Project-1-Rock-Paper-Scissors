1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click "Play the Game" button
3. Wait for the loading countdown (3 seconds)
4. Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors
5. See the computer's choice and round result
6. First to win 3 rounds wins the game!
7. Click "Play Again" to restart
