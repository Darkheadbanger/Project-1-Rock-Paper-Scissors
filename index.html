Welcome to my Rock Paper Scissors game

What are the rules

The rules are very simple:

  • Rock wins against Scissors but loses against Paper
  • Scissors wins against Paper but loses against Rock
  • Paper wins against Rock but loses against Scissors

Good advice

Want to win against the computer ? Try this strategy

  • If you played Rock, choose Scissors next
  • If you played Scissors, choose Paper next
  • If you played Paper, choose Rock next
